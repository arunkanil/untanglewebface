GrowCare

An iPad app for healthcare practitioners to conduct health screening at schools.



Growcare features an iPad app purpose built for screening basic health parameters of school-going students. The greater idea of Growcare is to eliminate as many preventable early age diseases as possible, through early detection.

The app handles tests on the students’ vision, hearing, oral health, and fundamental psychological health. After screening, the app generates a summarised health report for each student in the school, which can be shared with their parents; and a recommendation to consult a doctor can be made, if an anomaly is detected.
