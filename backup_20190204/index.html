The way we roll

We build apps for smartphones, tablets, and wearables.

We develop across smart devices, ensuring the best of mobility for your requirement.

So how do we do it? In 4 important stages:

1. Hearing you out

We discuss key aspects that will let us analyze your business's context better. At this stage, there’s just one thing we do- we diligently hear you out.

2. Fine-tuning the approach

We discuss your industry, your competition, your branding, your positioning; and most importantly, your intended users for the app. We shape solutions to specific challenges at your business. Together.

3. Prototyping

This is when we add that quintessential layer of technology all over the important stuff we discussed. The geeks at Untangle_ start with the internal vetting process. We carefully design and develop a working prototype or MVP (Minimum Viable Product).

4. Go-time

We Build, Test, Launch, Gather feedback. It’s lean agile 101. The product is extensively tested and made user ready. All set for feedback-sensitive updates, regular maintenance, and evolution.
