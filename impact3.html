H-Tec

An end-to-end lightweight property management system for small hotels.



H-Tec is an app developed for small format properties such as small hotels and lodges where pen and paper record keeping systems are still prevalent.

Including all the basic functions such as inventory bookings and allotment, H-Tec goes the extra mile by integrating an elaborate task management system that allows for timely generation of maintenance schedule, billing schedule and invoicing.
